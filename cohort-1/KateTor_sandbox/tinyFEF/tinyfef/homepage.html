A tiny front-end framework for displaying user interfaces.

Get started!

Simple

We like to keep everything we do simple, including our front-end solutions. You can take comfort in knowing that by using tinyFEF, you're not complicating the rest of your code base.

Declarative

Tell tinyFEF what you want to display, and it'll make it happen. There's no need to directly manipulate the DOM step-by-step when we can take care of that for you.

Tiny

It's in our name! No need to worry about code bloat, we've kept everything to the minimum. You can be confident that your users won't have to wait forever for your content to download.
